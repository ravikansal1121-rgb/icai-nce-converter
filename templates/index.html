How to use
  1. Select the constitution type (Proprietorship or Partnership)
  2. Optionally fill in client details (entity name, PAN, GSTIN, etc.)
  3. Prepare T-format financial statements in Excel (.xlsx), Word (.docx), or PDF (.pdf)
  4. Upload files for Current Year and Previous Year (or check "single file" if both years are in one file)
  5. Click "Convert" and download the auto-generated ICAI NCE format workbook
  6. Review the output, verify placeholders, and finalize
